Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a world-renowned dish known for its rich aroma and complex flavors. It consists of layers of fluffy basmati rice, tender marinated meat, and a blend of fragrant spices like saffron, cardamom, and star anise. Often served at celebrations, it is truly a complete meal in a single pot.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices: Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom, and Bay leaves
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves
  • A pinch of saffron soaked in warm milk

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the Basmati rice for 30 minutes, then parboil it with whole spices until 70% cooked.
  2. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and biryani spices for at least one hour.
  3. In a heavy-bottomed pot, fry the sliced onions until they are golden brown and crispy.
  4. Sauté the marinated chicken in the same pot until it is partially cooked.
  5. Layer the parboiled rice over the chicken, topping it with fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk.
  6. Seal the pot tightly and cook on low heat (Dum) for 20 minutes until the rice is fully steamed and the chicken is tender.
